## 1. Overview

This legal notice is provided in the spirit of transparency set out by the French
**loi n° 2004-575 du 21 juin 2004 pour la confiance dans l'économie numérique (LCEN)**,
even though **Open Omniscience** ("the Software") is **not** a public online
communication service operated by the Publisher: it is **free / open-source
software**, **installed and run locally by each user** on their own machine. The
Publisher **operates no server** accessible to the public and **collects no** user
data (see the [Privacy Policy](POLITIQUE_DE_CONFIDENTIALITE.md)).
